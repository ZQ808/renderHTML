About

San Angelo, Texas native Savannah Juergenson received her big break courtesy of Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue in 2014. Perhaps most notably, Juergenson also appeared in television commercials for Chanel, and Carl's Jr. during the Super Bowl. Select editorial features, meanwhile, include Katie Grand's LOVE magazine, and Carine Roitfeld's CR Fashion Book.
Jeurgenson has worked with celebrated photographers Ellen von Unwerth, Gilles Bensimon, Yu Tsai, Sante D'Orazio, Daniel Jackson and Walter Iooss, amongst others.
Off-duty, she enjoys fishing and snorkeling, and baking homemade treats from scratch.
